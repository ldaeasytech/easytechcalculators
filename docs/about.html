About EasyTech Calculators

EasyTech Calculators was created on December 11, 2025 to provide practical, reliable, and easy-to-use engineering and agricultural calculators for students, educators, engineers, agronomists, and technical professionals.

Many engineering calculations are repetitive, time-sensitive, and prone to manual error. This platform was built to simplify complex calculations while maintaining technical rigor, clarity, and real-world applicability.

Core Engineering & Agricultural Tools

EasyTech Calculators focuses on applied engineering tools that support real-world analysis, optimization, and design workflows.

  • Thermodynamics: Water & Steam Properties Calculator for complete thermodynamic and transport property evaluation.
  • Psychrometry & HVAC: Psychrometric Chart Calculator for moist air analysis including humidity ratio, wet-bulb temperature, dew point, enthalpy, specific volume, vapor pressure, and degree of saturation.
  • Pump & Fluid Flow Systems: Reynolds number, pipe friction (Darcy & Fanning), Moody diagram visualization, pump power, operating point analysis, NPSH evaluation, and economic pipe sizing.
  • Agricultural Engineering: Fertilizer Optimization Calculator for determining optimum N-P-K fertilizer combinations that meet crop nutrient requirements at minimum total cost.

Fertilizer Optimization – Minimum Cost Nutrient Formulation

The Fertilizer Optimization Calculator goes beyond simple nutrient balancing. It computes optimal fertilizer blends that satisfy nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K) requirements while minimizing total material and application cost.

This tool integrates nutrient mass balance equations with economic ranking, making it suitable for crop planning, soil test adjustments, and cost-efficient agricultural decision-making.

Psychrometric & Air Property Analysis

The Psychrometric Chart Calculator supports HVAC engineering, air-conditioning design, evaporative cooling analysis, and drying process evaluation.

Using established thermodynamic relationships (ASHRAE-based formulations), the calculator determines complete moist air properties from multiple independent input pairs, ensuring consistency with engineering standards.

Engineering Methodology & Accuracy

Each calculator is built using established engineering principles such as:

  • Mechanical energy balance equations
  • Darcy–Weisbach friction modeling
  • Pump affinity laws
  • Thermodynamic property correlations
  • Mass and energy balances
  • Economic cost optimization techniques

The objective is not only to generate numerical outputs, but to reflect proper engineering methodology and analytical reasoning.

Expanding Technical Coverage

EasyTech Calculators continues to expand into additional technical domains, including:

  • Drying and mass transfer processes
  • Evaporative cooling systems
  • Cooling tower performance evaluation
  • Additional thermodynamic and fluid mechanics utilities
  • Advanced agricultural and nutrient management tools

The long-term vision is to build a comprehensive, interdisciplinary engineering calculation platform covering core chemical, mechanical, HVAC, agricultural, and process engineering needs.

Responsible Use

While every effort is made to ensure numerical accuracy and correct implementation of formulas, all results should be independently verified before use in professional design, regulatory submission, or safety-critical applications.

EasyTech Calculators is intended for educational support, preliminary design, engineering analysis, and decision-making assistance.
