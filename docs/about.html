About EasyTech Calculators

EasyTech Calculators was created on December 11, 2025 to provide practical, reliable, and easy-to-use engineering calculators for students, educators, engineers, and technical professionals.

Many engineering calculations are repetitive, time-sensitive, and prone to manual error. This platform was built to simplify complex calculations while maintaining technical correctness and clarity.

Our Focus

EasyTech Calculators specializes in applied engineering tools that support real-world analysis and design.

  • Thermodynamics: Water & Steam property calculations
  • Pump & Fluid Flow Systems: Power requirements, operating point analysis, economic pipe sizing, NPSH evaluation, and gravity tank elevation design

The pump and fluid flow calculators are designed to cover the complete workflow of hydraulic system design — from energy requirements and cavitation prevention to economic optimization and performance evaluation.

What Makes This Platform Different

Each calculator is built using established engineering principles such as mechanical energy balance, Darcy-Weisbach friction analysis, pump affinity laws, and economic cost modeling.

The goal is not just to provide numbers, but to provide tools that reflect proper engineering methodology.

Expanding Engineering Coverage

EasyTech Calculators is continuously expanding into additional technical domains, including:

  • Agricultural engineering tools (e.g., fertilizer application calculators)
  • Psychrometric analysis and air-conditioning calculations
  • Drying and mass transfer processes
  • Additional fluid mechanics and thermodynamics utilities

The long-term vision is to build a comprehensive engineering calculation platform covering core mechanical, agricultural, and process engineering needs.

Responsible Use

While every effort is made to ensure numerical accuracy and correct implementation of formulas, all results should be independently verified before use in professional design or safety-critical applications.

EasyTech Calculators is intended for educational support, preliminary design, and engineering analysis assistance.
