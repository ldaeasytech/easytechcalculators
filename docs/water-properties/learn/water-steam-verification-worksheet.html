Water & Steam Property Verification Worksheet

Instructor-focused worksheet for verifying thermodynamic and transport properties of water and steam using multiple input pairs.

This worksheet is intended for instructional use. Students should compute properties using steam tables or handbooks. The calculator may be used by instructors to verify results.

Property Requirements

  • Single Phase & Saturated: density, specific gravity, enthalpy, entropy, Cv, Cp, thermal conductivity, viscosity
  • Mixed (Two-Phase): density, specific gravity, enthalpy, entropy only
