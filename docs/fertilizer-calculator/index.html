How to Calculate Fertilizer Requirements

What is NPK in Fertilizers?

NPK refers to Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K), the three primary nutrients required for plant growth.

Why Use Fertilizer Optimization?

Instead of applying a single fertilizer, combining multiple fertilizers allows farmers to meet exact nutrient requirements at lower cost.

How Does This Calculator Work?

The calculator solves three simultaneous nutrient equations (N, P, K) for 50 predefined fertilizer combinations and ranks them based on total cost.

Results should be verified by agricultural professionals before field application.
